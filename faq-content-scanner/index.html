What counts as a question-shaped heading

Any H2 or H3 that ends in "?" or opens with what, how, why, when, where, who, which, can, does, is, or are. We pull the heading plus the text immediately after it as a draft answer — read it, trim it, make sure it is actually true and actually on your page before you publish schema for it.
