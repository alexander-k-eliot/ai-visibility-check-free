Why this number, not just margin

A sale with a 60% margin that ate 90 minutes of sourcing, photos, listing, packing and messages can pay less per hour than a 30%-margin sale that took 10 minutes. Margin measures the sale. This measures your time — the number that actually decides whether reselling is beating an hourly job, or quietly not.

The 2026 US fee rates used here

  • Etsy: 6.5% transaction + 3% processing on price+shipping, + $0.25 processing + $0.20 listing.
  • eBay: ~13.25% final value fee on price+shipping + $0.30/order (most categories; category rates vary).
  • Poshmark: flat $2.95 under $15, otherwise 20% of price (buyers pay shipping).
  • Mercari: 10% flat on price+shipping.
  • Depop (US): 0% selling fee + 3.3% + $0.45 payment processing.

Rates verified July 2026. Platforms change fees — always verify in your seller account. This is a snapshot tool, not financial or tax advice.
