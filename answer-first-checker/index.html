What this actually checks

Three heuristics, stated plainly: (1) how many words appear before the first sentence ends, since a long windup usually means the answer is buried, (2) whether the paragraph opens with a hedge phrase ("in today's world," "it's important to note," "there are many factors") that delays the point, (3) when a question line precedes the paragraph, whether the first sentence shares any meaningful word with that question at all.
