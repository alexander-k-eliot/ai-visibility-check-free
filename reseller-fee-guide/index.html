The honest ranking, on fees alone

Depop keeps you the most per sale at this price point, Mercari and Etsy are close behind, eBay and Poshmark take the most. That is not the whole decision. Poshmark's buyer-pays-shipping model can net you more on heavy items. eBay's audience and search volume can mean more sales even at a lower per-sale keep. Fees are one input, not the whole answer.

The formulas, in full

  • Etsy: 6.5% transaction fee + 3% payment processing on (price + shipping), plus $0.25 payment processing and $0.20 per listing.
  • eBay: approximately 13.25% final value fee on (price + shipping) plus $0.30 per order. Exact rate varies by category.
  • Poshmark: flat $2.95 on sales under $15, otherwise 20% of the item price. Buyer pays shipping as a separate charge.
  • Mercari: 10% flat fee on (price + shipping).
  • Depop (US): 0% selling fee, plus 3.3% + $0.45 payment processing.

Rates verified July 2026 against each platform's published seller fee schedule. Platforms change fees, sometimes without much notice.
