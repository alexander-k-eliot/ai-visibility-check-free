FAQ Schema Generator

FAQPage markup lets AI assistants and search engines quote your answers directly instead of guessing which paragraph answers a question. Add your real Q&A, generate the code, paste it in your <head>. Free, runs in your browser.

Not sure what questions you already answer? Run the FAQ Content Scanner first — it finds question-shaped headings already on your page.

One rule that matters more than the code.

Only mark up questions you genuinely answer, visibly, on the same page — with matching wording. Mismatched schema is a policy violation for search engines and misleads AI assistants, the opposite of what we sell. Then add Organization schema, llms.txt, and a robots.txt AI-crawler policy to round out the basics, and verify with the free AI visibility checker.

Why this matters

Question-shaped headings with a clear answer are one of the strongest signals AI assistants use to extract quotable content.
