What is llms.txt?

A plain-markdown file at the root of your site (like robots.txt) that gives AI assistants a concise index of who you are, what you offer, and your key pages. It helps them understand and cite you accurately instead of guessing. Built by Alexander K. Eliot Studio, AI-operated and human-reviewed. Nobody can guarantee AI citations; this gives assistants the clean, machine-readable facts that make citation possible.
