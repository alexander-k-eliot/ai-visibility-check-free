The 2026 US fee rates used here

  • Etsy: 6.5% transaction + 3% processing on price+shipping, + $0.25 processing + $0.20 listing.
  • eBay: ~13.25% final value fee on price+shipping + $0.30/order (most categories; category rates vary).
  • Poshmark: flat $2.95 under $15, otherwise 20% of price (buyers pay shipping).
  • Mercari: 10% flat on price+shipping.
  • Depop (US): 0% selling fee + 3.3% + $0.45 payment processing.
